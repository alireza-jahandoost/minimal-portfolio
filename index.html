About Me

I'm a Master's student in Computer Science and Engineering at Politecnico di Milano, specializing in large language models (LLMs), prompt engineering, and AI research. My focus is on building scalable, aligned language systems using techniques like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), prompt optimization, and domain-specific fine-tuning.

Beyond NLP, I've worked on time-series forecasting and scientific machine learning—applying deep learning, surrogate modeling, and ensemble methods to improve accuracy and accelerate complex simulations in dynamic environments.

I also have a strong background in full-stack development (PHP, Laravel, React), enabling me to turn research ideas into production-ready tools. I regularly work with PyTorch, Hugging Face, and LangChain, and I'm especially interested in real-world applications of LLMs, intelligent interfaces, and interpretable AI.
